About Me
------
I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Communication and Information Theory ([CommIT](https://www.commit.tu-berlin.de/menue/aktuelles/)) group at the Technical University of Berlin, working with the Alexander von Humboldt Professor  [Prof. Giuseppe Caire](https://scholar.google.co.uk/citations?user=g66ErTcAAAAJ&hl=en). I received my M.Sc. in Communication Systems from Sharif University of Technology and my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Tehran (both in Tehran, Iran). 

Research Interests
------
I am broadly interested in various aspects of data processing and analysis in wireless communications, with an emphasis on massive MIMO systems. I use tools from applied mathematics to study the behaviour of high-dimensional signals arising in such systems and developing algorithms to process them. These tools include
* High-dimensional probability and statistics
* Convex Optimization
* Machine/Deep Learning
* Compressed Sensing and sparse recovery techniques
